WHO WE ARE

BEGINNING

We have started Techno 24/7 as Facebook page in 2013 and shortly after it became the favorite techno platform for many of the genre followers and professionals. Their incredible engagement helped us to become one of the main platforms in the scene. Today, we have 552.165 followers and 481.280 likes on the page!

Now we are expanding to other platforms, where we will provide journalistic articles, blogs and reviews. Also, fresh charts with new music , and crazy podcasts with amazing artists. We will produce and stream our own original content such as documentaries, interviews, behind the scenes reports, and many more.

FUTURE

We aim to become a leading platform in techno scene. Techno 24/7 owns some good content, and in constant lookout for more, to share with the army of our followers. This way we promote, completely free of charge, artists/events/brands/venues who are already on the radar, as well as those who deserve much more recognition.
